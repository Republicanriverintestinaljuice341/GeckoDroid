## 📱 Connecting your Android Device

GeckoDroid acts as a client for your Android device, which means you need to connect your device properly.

1. On your Android phone or tablet, enable **Developer Options**:
   - Open **Settings** > **About phone**.
   - Tap **Build number** seven times until you see a message saying Developer mode is on.
2. Go back to **Settings** > **Developer options**.
3. Enable **USB Debugging**.
4. Connect your Android device to your Windows PC with a USB cable.
5. When you connect, your device may ask to allow USB debugging. Tap **Allow**.

## ❓ Troubleshooting

If the app does not open or your device is not detected, try the following:

- Make sure your USB cable works for data transfer and not just charging.
- Re-enable USB debugging on your phone.
- Restart both your PC and Android device.
- Try a different USB port on your PC.
- Confirm Windows drivers for your device are installed.
- Run GeckoDroid as an administrator (right-click the app icon and pick **Run as administrator**).

If the problem continues, check the Issues page on the GitHub repository for help.
